August 2020 - Present

Overview

For my fourth-year Capstone project, myself and my team of peers are working with Aerlift Inc., a company that is creating a system that delivers crucial medical supplies from urban hospitals to rural clinics in Rwanda and the Democratic Republic of Congo. We are designing a drone that will be delivering these medical supplies (like blood for transfusions, vaccines, etc.), focussing on aerodynamics and structure while ensuring client needs are met and maximizing efficiency. We are supported by the Centre for Global Engineering within the University of Toronto.
