Overview

Supporting the LGBTQ+ community is important to the engineers at U of T, and every year we build a float and march in the Toronto Pride Parade. These floats are designed by the engineering students and we build them on the beds of 16-wheelers, so they're gigantic! It's a multi-day endeavour to build and paint the 2x4 structure and side panelling, plus the balloons, mirrors, lights or spinning stars that give the pizzazz that makes Pride so fun.
