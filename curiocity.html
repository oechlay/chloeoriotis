Overview

In my Science and Technology in Popular Media class, my friend and I covered the intricacies of social media represenations of women in science, technology, engineering and math (STEM) in our podcast, Curiocity. We interviewed a woman who's in STEM and active on social media, and had a conversation on the challenges women face from the public when posting about their lives in their professions. I'm pursuing a certificate in communication, and as a woman in STEM, working on this podcast has shown me the importance of authenticity and confidence in oneself in daily life and online.
