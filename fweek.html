2017, 2019, 2020

Overview

I was a Head Leader for the frosh group Delta during Frosh (Orientation) Week 2017, Kappa in 2019, and the all-new group Iota in 2020. I worked with my Co-HLs to build a 30-student leader team, a group "brand" and atmosphere over the summers, and lead a frosh group of over 60 first year engineering students through the events of each Orientation Week. This means I've been able to introduce almost 200 first-year students to our engineering community over my time as a Head Leader!

Being a Head Leader is special; I was the first point of contact and friendly face for the newest members of our school. University can be an intimidating new experience, and I took my job seriously as I guided the first-years through the challenges of the beginning of their undergrad journeys. This included encouraging extracurricular involvement, providing academic aid, and just being someone to talk to. Meeting so many new faces has been a highlight of my undergrad!
