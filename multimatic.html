Overview

I performed concept design and mechanism development in my position at MTC. My work included building 3D CAD models, performing FEA and topology optimizations, creating and updating drawings, tolerance stacks and kinematic studies, prototyping and evaluating assemblies, and designing fixtures for performing tests.
