2020

Overview

I was a castmember in U of T Engineering's Annual Sketch Comedy Musical Revue, Skule Nite 2T0. Now in its 100th year, Skule Nite welcomes the Engineering community and beyond to Hart House Theatre for a night of music and laughter, courtesy of an all-student cast and crew. I auditioned for Cast in September 2019, and through seven months of rehearsals, I pushed myself to develop my singing, dancing, and acting skills and prove I am capable of delivering a high-caliber performance in front of a live audience of 450 people during our three-night run. Due to the Covid-19 pandemic, the show was filmed and aired online for an audience of over 1000 people to make up for the remaining live shows that were cancelled.
