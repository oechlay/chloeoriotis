I'm Chloe Oriotis (OH-ree-OH-tis, pronounced like the cookie!), a mechanical engineer finishing my last year of undergrad at the Univeristy of Toronto. I'm specializing in mechatronics and bioengineering and pursuing a certificate in communication, and I've also worked in aerospace, automotive, and education. I'm passionate about leadership development, product design engineering and lifelong learning.

This website isn't just a portfolio, it's a project I took on to teach myself a new skill: web dev! I learned HTML and CSS to build the site and GitHub to bring it to life online. Turns out web dev is really fun!

I designed this site to display my technical AND soft skills, while keeping it bright and full of life. It's a summation of the big things I've done in my university career, from work experience to in-class projects to student government to musical theatre... There's a lot I've done that I'm proud of and I'm excited to share it with you!
