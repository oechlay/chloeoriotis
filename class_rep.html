2018-2020

Overview

I was the voted-in representative of my class to the faculty in the Mechanical and Industrial Engineering department. My main role was to be a liason between the students and the faculty, expressing any student desires or complaints that the faculty should be made aware of. I met with the faculty bi-weekly to update them on the class' academic experiences and to help the faculty improve their student support. I also organized community-building events and the Iron Ring party for the graduating Mechanical Engineers.
