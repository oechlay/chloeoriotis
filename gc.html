2018

Overview

Godiva's Crown is Skule's Spirit Head, tasked with bringing engineering spirit to all 5500 engineers at U of T. I performed a skit, sang, and did a comedy routine in front of hundreds of my peers as part of the competition to win the Crown, and was voted in by a panel of student judges (including the previous year's Godiva's Crown) to win.

During my year as GC, I attended as many Skule events as possible, including dinner dances, graduation ceremonies, and Toronto's Pride and Santa Claus Parades to spread engineering cheer. I also raised almost $600 in the name of Skule for Holland Bloorview Hospital's Capes for Kids fundraiser with my friend and Co-Spirit Head, Mr. Blue and Gold.
