Overview

I received a George Brown College Basic Machining Certificate after taking their course in second year. In the program I machined a functioning pneumatic piston system that can also be hand cranked.

Skills Used

I developed fundamental machining skills like lathing, drill pressing and tapping. I also learned the safety precautions that are needed to be taken in machine shops with such heavy equipment.

What I Learned

Machining straight holes and fine details is difficult! It took many tries to perfect the delicate parts of the piston like its thin rod.
